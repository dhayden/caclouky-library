The Problem

The library holds a growing collection of Bro. Sowders' sermon papers and teachings in PDF format. These documents contain invaluable theological content — but today there is no way to search them meaningfully.

"What did Bro. Sowders teach about long hair?"

A member wanting to research a topic must open and read through each PDF manually. This makes the existing material largely inaccessible for everyday use and research.

The Solution

Add an AI-powered chat search to the Caclouky Library. Members type a question in plain English — the system reads the relevant portions of Bro. Sowders' sermon PDFs and returns a clear, conversational answer with citations showing the exact document and page the answer came from.


This is not a keyword search. The AI understands the meaning of a question and finds relevant content even when the exact words don't match — it reasons over the source material the same way a researcher would.

How It Works — The RAG Pipeline (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
1

PDF Upload

Admin uploads Bro. Sowders' sermon PDFs via the library admin panel. Files are stored on the server.

2

Text Extraction

Text is extracted from each PDF page automatically by the API server.

3

Chunking

Text is split into ~500-word sections, each tagged with document name and page number.

4

AI Indexing

OpenAI converts each chunk into a numeric "fingerprint" of its meaning (embedding), stored in the database.

5

Semantic Search

Member's question is also embedded → top 5 most relevant chunks retrieved from the database.

6

AI Answer

Claude AI reads the retrieved chunks and writes a natural language answer with page citations.

What did Bro. Sowders teach about long hair?
AI Answer
Bro. Sowders taught that long hair on women is a symbol of their submission to God and their husbands, drawing from 1 Corinthians 11. He emphasized that a woman's uncut hair serves as her "covering" and is a sign of spiritual identity in the church. He also addressed that men cutting their hair short reflects the headship order established by God.

Did he give a scripture reference for this?
AI Answer
Yes — Bro. Sowders primarily referenced 1 Corinthians 11:3–15, citing verse 15 specifically: "But if a woman have long hair, it is a glory to her: for her hair is given her for a covering." He used this passage as the scriptural foundation throughout his teaching on this subject.
